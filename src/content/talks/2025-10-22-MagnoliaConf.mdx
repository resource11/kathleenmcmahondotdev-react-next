In this talk, I will share my experience of creating type-safe design systems in React. I will discuss the benefits of using TypeScript for design systems and how it can help you create more robust and maintainable design systems. I will also provide practical tips and best practices for creating type-safe design systems that can help you ensure that your design system is easy to use and maintain for all users.
