In this panel discussion, I will join other industry experts to discuss the pros and cons of design systems. We will explore the benefits of design systems, such as consistency and efficiency, as well as the potential drawbacks, such as rigidity and lack of creativity. We will also discuss how to determine whether a design system is necessary for a particular project and how to implement a design system effectively if it is deemed necessary. This panel will provide valuable insights for developers and designers who are considering implementing a design system in their projects.
