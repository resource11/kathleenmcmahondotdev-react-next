In this talk, I will share my experience of creating a design system with a focus on inclusivity. I will discuss the challenges I faced and the strategies I used to ensure that the design system is accessible and welcoming to all users, regardless of their background or experience level. I will also provide practical tips and best practices for creating inclusive design systems that can help other developers create more inclusive products.
