In this workshop, I will share my experience of creating accessible React components using the Sand Dollar approach. I will discuss the principles of the Sand Dollar approach and how it can help you create more accessible and inclusive React components. I will also provide practical tips and best practices for implementing the Sand Dollar approach in your React projects to ensure that your components are accessible to all users.
