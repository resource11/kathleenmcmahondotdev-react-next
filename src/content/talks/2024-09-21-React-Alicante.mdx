In this lightning talk, I will share my experience of using Storybook to create accessible design system tests. I will discuss the benefits of using Storybook for testing and how it can help you create more accessible and inclusive design systems. I will also provide practical tips and best practices for using Storybook to create accessible design system tests that can help you ensure that your design system is accessible to all users.
