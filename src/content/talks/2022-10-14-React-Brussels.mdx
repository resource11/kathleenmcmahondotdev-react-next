In this talk, I will share my experience of using feature flags to rebrand a product in secret. I will discuss the challenges I faced and the strategies I used to ensure that the rebranding process was smooth and successful. I will also provide practical tips and best practices for using feature flags to manage a rebranding process that can help other developers create more successful rebranding efforts.
