In this talk, I will share my experience of using Astro and MDX to create a digital garden. I will discuss the benefits of using these technologies for content creation and how they can help you create a more engaging and interactive digital garden. I will also provide practical tips and best practices for using Astro and MDX to create a digital garden that can help you cultivate your ideas and share them with the world.
